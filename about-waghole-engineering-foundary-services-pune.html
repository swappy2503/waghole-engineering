About

We are Foundary Top Services That We Offers

As the one of the Oldest Aluminium casting foundries in the INDIA, with satisfied clients , Waghole Engineering has built a strong reputation in the casting industry. We work with numerous well-known Tier 1’s & OEM’s manufacturers supplying raw and fully machined aluminium castings for many applications. From our large modern manufacturing facilities located in Bhosari Pune in the India , our engineers are dedicated to delivering projects from conception with a commitment to quality. At our Aluminium foundry we have the capabilities to produce quality castings from 0.100 gm to 100 kgs for customers in a diverse range of industries including:

  • Power Sector
  • Machine Tools
  • Valves
  • Medical Equipments
  • Light Construction Equipment
  • Actuator
  • Automobile
  • General Engineering

Key Strengths

Waghole Engineering is one of the strongest aluminium foundries in the market due to a number of key business strengths:

  • We have over 20 years of experience in the manufacture of high quality aluminium castings up to 150 kg in weight.
  • Continued investment is the core principle behind our development with constant upgrading of in-house capabilities, new technology and people skills.
  • Our foundry caters to the requirements of many industries, including Power Sector Machine Tools , Valves, Medical Equipment , Light Construction Equipment , Actuator ,Automobile ,General Engineering
  • We benefit from extensive processes and technical capabilities, including production cells, efficient finishing facilities, shot blast, heat treatment, fully equipped laboratory and extensive metal control including rotary degassing.
  • Complete project support with comprehensive development, tool making and prototyping facilities are also available to all customers.
  • Our business prides itself in its experienced, technically capable and customer focused team.

Working to strict international standards, and fully accredited to ISO 9001:2015, we pride ourselves on being one of the world’s leading sand casting companies.

We assure you that we shall successfully develop any type of jobs to your satisfaction within the shortest span of time.

In addition, we are having well developed machine shop, all types testing’s, coatings and other related facilities as well.
